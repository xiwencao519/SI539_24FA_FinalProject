Meet the Characters of Siam LIP

The "Four Meats and One Veggie" Family

Siam LIP

LIP is a siamese cat with a quirky and lively personality. His MBTI is the hybrid of ENTP and ESTP.

Beagle Dodong

The cheeky troublemaker dog known for his playful and chaotic energy. He's always cooking up a new scheme!

Rabbit Xiuzhen

A warm-hearted yet reserved rabbit. She's the balance in the group, offering wisdom and a cool perspective.

Zhang Tianzhong

The perpetually emo carrot. Zhang embodies the quiet, reflective side of life with a hint of existential humor.

Pelican Xiaohe

The joyful and simple pelican. Xiaohe's innocence and unfiltered joy make him the heart of the group.
